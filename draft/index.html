About California Global TS Company

At California Global TS Company, we believe in fashion with a purpose. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to transform surplus apparel into sustainable style statements.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to redefine the fashion industry's approach to excess inventory. By rescuing high-quality garments from top brands, we're not just offering incredible style at affordable prices; we're also contributing to a greener planet.

Sustainable Practices

Our commitment to sustainability goes beyond the clothes we sell. We meticulously select each piece to ensure it meets our standards of quality and eco-friendliness. Through our efforts, we aim to reduce the environmental impact of the fashion industry, one garment at a time.
